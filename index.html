Featured Speaker

Joe Dispenza

Doctor of chiropractic, international speaker, and writer

Joe Dispenza has visited over 33 countries to teach people to create a happier and healthier life.He is also a bestselling author; his books include "You Are The Placebo" and "Breaking The Habit of Being Yourself."

Simon Sinek

British-born American author and inspirational speaker

Sinek has written five books. "Start With Why", his first book, was published October 2009. His second book, titled "Leaders Eat Last", appeared on the bestseller lists of the Wall Street Journal and The New York Times

Tony Robbins

American author, coach, speaker, and philanthropist

Robbins is known for his infomercials, seminars, and self-help books including the books "Unlimited Power" and "Awaken the Giant Within".

Lewis Howes

Entrepreneur, performance business coach, YouTuber and keynote speaker

Lewis Howes hosts a top 100 iTunes ranked podcast, "The School of Greatness", is a New York Times bestselling author of "The School of Greatness" and "The Mask of Masculinity".Details magazine called him one of “5 Internet Guru’s that can Make You Rich.”

Jay Shetty

British podcaster, author, and life coach

Shetty is a co-founder of Sama Tea.He has written "Think Like a Monk".The book was a bestseller in 2023.He published “8 Rules of Love: How to Find It, Keep It and Let It Go” which made the New York Times best-seller list.

Robert Greene

American author of books on strategy, power, and seduction

Greene has written six international bestsellers, including "The 48 Laws of Power", "The Art of Seduction", "The 33 Strategies of War", "The 50th Law", "Mastery", and "The Laws of Human Nature".
